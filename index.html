About Joan International

Innovative Startup Trading Excellence

Joan International Trading Company is an innovative startup trading firm with proven expertise in sourcing, marketing, and delivering commodities to customers worldwide. Our foundation is built on trust, operational excellence, and a commitment to responsible global trade.

With strategic partnerships across key markets and deep relationships spanning producers, logistics networks, and end-users, we are uniquely positioned to deliver efficient supply solutions in today's dynamic markets.

Our Journey

25 Years of Growth & Excellence

2000

Foundation & Vision

Joan International Trading Company established with a mission to connect global commodity markets with operational excellence and integrity.

2005

Expansion to GCC Markets

Entered energy and industrial commodity markets across the GCC. Secured long-term offtake contracts with major regional buyers.

2010

Metals & Minerals Division Launch

Expanded commodity portfolio to include metals and minerals. Opened operations in Africa and strengthened Asia-Pacific presence.

2015

Supply Chain Solutions Platform

Launched integrated supply chain solutions. Expanded to 20+ countries with advanced logistics and financing capabilities.

2018

Risk & Market Intelligence Unit

Established advanced trading strategies and market analytics division. Introduced hedging solutions for commodity price risk.

2021

Sustainability Commitment

Launched ESG and sustainability initiatives. Committed to net-zero emissions roadmap and community development programs.

2025

Global Network & Innovation

Operating in 30+ countries with 500+ staff worldwide. Pioneering metering solutions and digital supply chain transparency.
